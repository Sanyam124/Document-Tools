About Us

Welcome to the Document Tools Platform, your go-to solution for efficient text extraction and document processing. Our mission is to provide easy-to-use, powerful tools that help you extract, manage, and manipulate text from a variety of document formats.

Our Features

We currently offer tools that enable you to perform Optical Character Recognition (OCR) on images and PDFs, allowing you to convert scanned documents or image-based text into editable, readable formats. Our tools are simple, fast, and designed to save you time.

Our Vision

Our goal is to continue expanding our platform, adding more functionalities that make document management tasks easier, faster, and more accurate. We strive to help students, professionals, and businesses automate and streamline their document-related workflows.
